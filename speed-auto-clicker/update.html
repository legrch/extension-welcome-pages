VERSION 3.0

What's New

Speed Auto Clicker just got a major upgrade. Here's what changed.

Floating Widget
A new draggable control panel on your pages. Start, stop, and add click points without opening the popup. Hover to reveal, drag to reposition.
Multi-Point Clicking
Click up to 100 different points on a page. Add points visually with crosshair selection, drag markers to reposition, delete individually or all at once.
Pause & Resume
Pause the clicker at any time and resume exactly where you left off. Your click count and points stay intact.
Iframe Support
Click inside embedded frames and iframes on supported websites.
Per-Site Settings
Your click interval and jitter preferences are now saved per website. Switch between sites and your settings follow automatically.

Open any website and press Alt+Z to show the widget, or use the extension popup.
