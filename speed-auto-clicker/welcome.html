ALWAYS ON SCREEN
Floating Widget
A glassmorphic control panel that lives on your page. Start, stop, add click points, and view live stats — without ever opening the popup. Drag it anywhere you want.
PRECISION TARGETING
Multi-Point Clicking
Place up to 100 click targets on any page using crosshair selection. Each target ripples when clicked. Drag markers to reposition. Delete individually or clear all at once.
FULL CONTROL
Popup Control Center
Click the extension icon to open the popup — your command center. Set the click interval from 0.1s to any value, toggle Humanize mode, manage points, view real-time statistics, and start/stop with one tap.

Pause & Resume
