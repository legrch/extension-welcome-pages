Everything You Can Do

Speed Auto Clicker is packed with features designed to save you time and clicks.

Floating Widget
A draggable glass control panel that lives on your page. Start, stop, add points, and view stats — all without opening the popup. Drag it anywhere you want.
Multi-Point Clicking
Place up to 100 click targets on any page. Use crosshair selection to visually place each point. Drag markers to reposition. Delete individually or clear all at once.
Adjustable Click Interval
Set the time between clicks — from blazing fast (0.1 seconds) to as slow as you need. Adjust with +/- buttons, type a value, or use the widget's inline controls.
Humanize Mode
Adds ±10% random variation to each click interval, so your clicks look like a real person — not a bot. Toggle it on in the popup or settings page.
Pause & Resume
Pause the clicker at any moment, then resume right where you left off. Your click count, cycle count, and point positions stay intact.
Drag to Reposition
Move click points after placing them — just drag any crosshair marker to a new spot. The widget panel itself is also fully draggable.
Per-Site Settings
Your click interval and humanize preferences are saved per website. Switch between sites and your settings follow automatically.
Iframe Support
Click inside embedded frames and iframes on supported websites. The clicker automatically relays clicks across frame boundaries.
Keyboard Shortcuts
Start/stop the clicker, add click points, show the widget, and open the popup — all from your keyboard. Fully customizable in Chrome's extension settings.
Live Statistics
Track your points, cycles, total clicks, and elapsed time in real-time — right on the widget and in the popup. Always know exactly what the clicker is doing.
Tab Badge
See the clicker's state at a glance. The extension badge shows a cyan dot when running, amber when paused, and the point count in violet when adding targets.
Throttle Protection
If the browser throttles your tab (e.g. when minimized), the clicker warns you so you know clicks may be delayed. No silent failures.
