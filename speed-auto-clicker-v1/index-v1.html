Speed Auto Clicker Installed

Follow these steps to get started.

1 Getting Started

Pin to Your Toolbar

Click the puzzle piece icon in Chrome's toolbar to see your extensions. Then click the pin icon next to Speed Auto Clicker.

Once pinned, the extension icon stays visible in your toolbar for quick access.
2 Using the Extension

Open and Configure

Click the extension icon to open the auto clicker panel. Set your preferred click interval, assign a hotkey, and start auto clicking.
